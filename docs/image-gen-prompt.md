# Image Generation Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, tuning charts, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 studio product photograph of a finished tunable snare frame array: three shallow steel frame drums of graduated diameter mounted side-by-side in a shared plasma-cut cold-rolled steel bracket frame, with standard white drum heads, individual lug hardware, small snare wires or throw-off mechanisms, and a clean horizontal playing layout. The largest drum sits on one side, medium in the center, smallest on the other, all slightly proud of the bracket with visible standoff ears and rim hardware. Materials should read as satin steel, chrome lugs, white Mylar heads, and dark snare hardware. Clean studio background, soft directional studio lighting, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic rehearsal/workshop scene of the tunable snare frame array clamped on a stand or held at table height while a player uses light sticks or brushes across the three drum heads. Show a drum key, spare heads, and a small tuner on a nearby bench to suggest independent tuning and validation. Natural light, realistic percussionist posture, clear view of the shared steel bracket, graduated shell depths, and separate snare controls.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of one drum module in the array: rolled cold-rolled steel shell, white head seated under a small rim, lug rod and tension hardware, snare wire crossing the underside or edge, and the welded or bolted standoff ear that connects it to the shared bracket. Shallow depth of field, soft side light, high detail, believable deburred metal and drum hardware.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, labels, watermarks, brand marks, impossible snare paths, extra limbs, warped circular heads, random cymbals, cutaway dimension callouts, DXF-like line art, or fabrication measurements; hands must be anatomically plausible if shown.
