# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished tunable snare frame array: three small snare-style frame drums of graduated diameter — large, medium, and small — mounted side-by-side in a single brushed cold-rolled steel mounting bracket, the bracket a flat plate with three circular cut-outs and crisp 90-degree flanged top and bottom edges for stiffness. Each drum is a shallow rolled-steel cylinder with a white translucent Mylar drum head stretched over the top rim, held by a thin steel counter-hoop pulled down by short hooked tension rods seating into small black claw lugs welded around each shell exterior — four lugs on the smallest drum, six on the larger two. The graduated diameters create a clear stair-step silhouette across the array; total form reads as a compact hand-percussion kit roughly half a meter wide and a hand-span tall. Materials read honestly: silver brushed steel shells and bracket, slightly darker steel tone on the frame, matte white heads, black hardware. Clean neutral light-gray studio background, soft directional studio light raking across the brushed metal, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.
