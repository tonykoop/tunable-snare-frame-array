# Tunable Snare Frame Array — Starter Playbook

Your instrument is a 3-drum snare array tuned to **D–A–D** (open power chord):

```
Drum A  10-inch (250 mm OD)  →  D3  ≈ 147 Hz  (bass drone)
Drum B   8-inch (200 mm OD)  →  A3  ≈ 220 Hz  (mid voice)
Drum C   6-inch (150 mm OD)  →  D4  ≈ 294 Hz  (treble lead)
```

All three drums share a single steel mounting frame. Play the array as a
single instrument — left hand handles the outer drums (A and C), right
hand plays the center drum (B), or reverse to taste.

Snare wires can be engaged (buzzy, snare texture) or disengaged (clean
membrane thump) with each drum's thumb-wheel release. This gives you
**six tone flavors** from three drums.

## Difficulty key
- ★☆☆ — Beginner: single-drum taps, basic pulse patterns
- ★★☆ — Intermediate: 2-drum cross patterns, snare on/off interplay
- ★★★ — Advanced: all 3 drums, polyrhythm, snare-weave

## Playing technique
| Stroke | Zone | Sound |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Center tap** | Middle of head | Fundamental pitch, full resonance |
| **Edge tap** | 10–20 mm from rim | Brighter, higher partial, shorter decay |
| **Slap** | Full palm, lift fast | Deep "thud", muted |
| **Rim click** | Strike rim only | High, dry click tone |
| **Finger roll** | Alternating fingertips, rapid | Sustained texture/roll |

## Snare vocabulary
| Setting | Thumb-wheel | Sound character |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Full engage** | Tightened in | Full buzz on every stroke |
| **Half engage** | Mid-range | Occasional ghost snare, subtle grit |
| **Disengaged** | Released out | Clean membrane tone |
